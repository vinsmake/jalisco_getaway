Guadalajara

It is known for tequila and mariachi music, both born in Jalisco, state of which Guadalajara is the capital. The historic center of Guadalajara has colonial squares and emblematic places such as the neoclassical Degollado Theater and a cathedral with twin golden needles. The Government Palace houses famous murals by the painter José Clemente Orozco.
